Platform

Four layers.
One platform.

Sentinel is not a dashboard bolted onto a sensor. It is a purpose-built pipeline — each layer engineered for its role, integrated end-to-end, deployed entirely within your infrastructure. No external services. No data leaving your perimeter.

Layer 01
Edge Agent
A lightweight binary that connects to your existing hardware — Modbus TCP, CAN Bus, MQTT, OPC-UA, Serial, Syslog, and more. No proprietary hardware required. Deploy once, connect anything. Fully configurable from the SHIP dashboard without touching the agent.
11 connectors
↓ normalized signal stream
Layer 02
SHIP Gateway
The secure transport and normalization layer. SHIP receives raw signal frames from the Edge Agent, validates and normalizes them into a unified evidence schema, enforces access control, and routes structured data to the analysis engine. Your single pane of glass for signal operations.
Real-time dashboard
↓ classified signal events
Layer 03
Hermes Engine
The deterministic analysis core. Hermes evaluates every signal event against 14 behavior classes — scoring severity, stability, timing, and anomaly intensity without probabilistic drift. Same input always produces the same output. Auditable, reproducible, defensible.
Deterministic · 14 classes
↓ evidence-grade incidents
Layer 04
Evidence Reporter
Every meaningful anomaly becomes a structured incident with full context — timestamps, signal chain, severity score, behavior classification, and decision record. Export as PDF. Archive for audit. 30-day on-premise retention with configurable policies.
PDF · Audit-ready

Coverage

11 signal types.
Zero blind spots.

Most monitoring tools are built for one domain. Sentinel covers all of them under a single evidence model. If your facility produces a signal, Sentinel can ingest, analyze, and preserve it as evidence.

CAN Bus

Bus load, timing anomalies, replay patterns, frame integrity, arbitration stress, and transport-layer irregularities.

Modbus RTU

CRC integrity, missed polling, illegal function codes, stuck registers, timeout storms, rogue writes, and bus contention patterns.

Audio

Dropouts, clipping, flatline behavior, gain instability, channel imbalance, and audible signal degradation signatures.

Biometric

Heart rate variability, oxygen saturation drift, timing gaps, sensor instability, and behavior-level biological anomaly patterns.

EEG / ECG

Arrhythmia patterns, electrode dropout, signal saturation, timing inconsistency, waveform behavior shifts, and artifact detection.

IoT Sensor

Out-of-range values, dead sensors, stuck values, burst polling, unstable transitions, and low-trust source behavior.

RF / IQ

Carrier drift, jamming behavior, IQ imbalance, SNR collapse, spectrum instability, and degraded radio signal confidence.

Vibration

Imbalance, bearing failure, resonance signatures, kurtosis shifts, ISO-style zone behavior, and physical degradation mapping.

Lab Science

Instrument drift, contamination spikes, calibration loss, baseline deviation, unstable measurement behavior, and evidence traceability.

Serial

Framing errors, overflow patterns, baud mismatch, line noise, packet loss, and transport instability across constrained links.

Network

Packet loss, latency anomalies, TTL instability, flood behavior, port scan patterns, and protocol-level signal intelligence built for modern operations.
