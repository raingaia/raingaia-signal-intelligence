Coverage

11 signal types.
One premium engine.

Hermes is not a single-purpose dashboard. It is a multi-domain signal intelligence platform built to normalize, classify, score, and export evidence across industrial, medical, lab, telecom, serial, and network contexts.

CAN Bus

Bus load, timing anomalies, replay patterns, frame integrity, arbitration stress, and transport-layer irregularities.

Modbus RTU

CRC integrity, missed polling, illegal function codes, stuck registers, timeout storms, rogue writes, and bus contention patterns.

Audio

Dropouts, clipping, flatline behavior, gain instability, channel imbalance, and audible signal degradation signatures.

Biometric

Heart rate variability, oxygen saturation drift, timing gaps, sensor instability, and behavior-level biological anomaly patterns.

EEG / ECG

Arrhythmia patterns, electrode dropout, signal saturation, timing inconsistency, waveform behavior shifts, and artifact detection.

IoT Sensor

Out-of-range values, dead sensors, stuck values, burst polling, unstable transitions, and low-trust source behavior.

RF / IQ

Carrier drift, jamming behavior, IQ imbalance, SNR collapse, spectrum instability, and degraded radio signal confidence.

Vibration

Imbalance, bearing failure, resonance signatures, kurtosis shifts, ISO-style zone behavior, and physical degradation mapping.

Lab Science

Instrument drift, contamination spikes, calibration loss, baseline deviation, unstable measurement behavior, and evidence traceability.

Serial

Framing errors, overflow patterns, baud mismatch, line noise, packet loss, and transport instability across constrained links.

Network

Packet loss, latency anomalies, TTL instability, flood behavior, port scan patterns, and protocol-level signal intelligence built for modern operations.

Platform Architecture

Production-ready.
Evidence-first.

Hermes is designed as a premium operational platform: ingest, normalize, classify, score, and export. It is built not only to detect anomalies, but to preserve them as evidence users can download, review, and act on.

01

Multi-signal normalization

Each signal type is normalized through a dedicated path so diverse sources can be evaluated inside one unified evidence model.

02

Behavior-class scoring

Hermes does not stop at raw alerts. It evaluates signal behavior, severity, stability, confidence, timing, and anomaly intensity.

03

OracleEvidenceFrame output

Every meaningful event can be converted into a structured evidence object for diagnostics, reporting, and downstream decision workflows.

04

PDF-ready reporting

Users do not need to memorize console logs. Hermes supports downloadable, portable evidence outputs for review, audit, and action.

05

Industrial-grade visibility

From Modbus and CAN Bus to RF, medical, lab, and network signals, the platform keeps critical systems visible under one premium interface.

06

Enterprise presentation

Designed to look and behave like a real product, not a lab prototype: premium UI, clear sections, credible structure, and commercial readiness.
